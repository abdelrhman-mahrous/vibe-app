# 🌙 Vibe App

> **Enter a world of focus, calm, and premium experience**

**Vibe** is a modern mobile application designed to help users relax, focus, and manage their daily tasks using immersive ambient sounds and simple productivity tools.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎧 Ambient Sound Experience

* Rain sounds 🌧️
* White noise 🤍
* Ocean & forest sounds 🌊🌲
* Layered sound mixing

---

### 🎚️ Sound Mixer

* Combine multiple sounds together
* Control individual volume levels
* Dynamic waveform visualization

---

### 🔗 External Audio Support

* Play audio from:

  * YouTube links
  * Podcasts
  * Local device files
* Smooth playback experience

---

### ⏳ Pomodoro Timer

* Focus mode (25 min)
* Short & long breaks
* Animated circular timer
* Background sound integration

---

### ✅ To-Do List

* Add, delete, complete tasks
* Swipe gestures
* Minimal and clean UI

---

### 🎵 Audio Player

* Floating mini player across screens
* Smooth transitions between tracks
* Fade in / fade out effects

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

* 🌑 Dark mode only
* 🎯 Minimal & distraction-free
* 💎 Premium and elegant UI
* ✨ Glassmorphism + soft gradients
* 🎬 Smooth animations & micro-interactions

---

## 🌍 Arabic Experience

* Full Arabic support (RTL)
* Modern Arabic typography
* Clean and readable UI
* Elegant microcopy

---

## 🧠 User Experience

* Simple and intuitive
* Built for long sessions
* Focus on calmness and flow
